### Cutting-Edge Research

1.  **Nvidia Open-Sources General Gaming Foundation Model NitroGen.**
    Nvidia has released the [NitroGen model (AI News)](https://www.jiqizhixin.com/articles/2025-12-21-11), capable of playing over 1000 games. Based on the GR00T architecture, it only needs fine-tuning to pick up new games. This isn't just a script; it's a general-purpose large model with cross-game generalization capabilities, learning from controller operations in internet videos. It doesn't require deep thinking, focusing instead on intuitive player-style [Motion Control (AI News)](https://nitrogen.minedojo.org), paving the way for general embodied AI.

2.  **Anthropic Releases SGTM Safety Isolation Technology.**
    Anthropic has introduced [SGTM technology (AI News)](https://www.jiqizhixin.com/articles/2025-12-21-11), which can remove dangerous knowledge without deleting data. This method isolates sensitive information like CBRN via gradient routing. Utilizing an adsorption effect, even unlabeled hazardous content can automatically cluster into specific parameter regions, which can then be zeroed out with a single click. Compared to traditional filtering, it's more robust and retains general capabilities, representing a [New Paradigm (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.05648) for safety research.
